## What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

**GEO** is the practice of optimizing content for AI-powered search engines and chatbots that generate answers rather than just listing links. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in search results, GEO ensures your content is:

1. **Easily extractable** by AI models
2. **Clearly structured** with factual information
3. **Conversational** and natural language-friendly
4. **Rich in schema markup** for machine understanding
5. **Citable** with clear sources and statistics
